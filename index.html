Privacy Policy

Privy — Journal · Last updated 7 June 2026

Privy is a private journaling app. It is built so that your writing stays yours. This policy explains, in plain language, what data the app handles and what it does not.

The short version

  • There is no account and no login. We never ask for your name, email, or any identifier.
  • We operate no servers and run no analytics, tracking, or advertising.
  • Your entries are stored on your device, and — if you enable it — in your own private iCloud account.
  • We, the developer, cannot see your entries. Ever.

What the app stores

Everything you create in Privy — journal entries, titles, photos, voice notes, tags, vaults, and reminders — is saved locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage.

iCloud sync

If iCloud is enabled on your device, Privy can sync your entries across your own Apple devices using Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) in your private iCloud database. This data is tied to your Apple ID and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. The developer of Privy has no access to it and no ability to read it.

Passwords, passcode & Face ID

Optional app passcodes and per-note / per-vault passwords are stored in Apple's Keychain on your device (and, if you turn on iCloud Keychain, synced privately by Apple). Face ID / Touch ID is handled entirely by iOS; Privy never receives your biometric data.

Notifications

Reminders are scheduled as local notifications on your device. No reminder content leaves your device or passes through any server.

Third parties

Privy contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only external service involved is Apple iCloud, and only if you choose to enable it.

Deleting your data

You are always in control. You can delete any entry at any time, and Settings → Data → Delete all data permanently erases every entry, vault, tag, password, and your profile from your device and from your iCloud. This action cannot be undone.

Children

Privy is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
